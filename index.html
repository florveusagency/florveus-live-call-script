Common Objections

"I only have a few minutes"

"Perfect! I actually only have [less time than they mentioned], so let's get started on finding you a better rate right away. Can you verify your address for me?"

If still hesitant:

"I understand your time is valuable. Let me just quickly show you your savings - it'll only take 2 minutes. Would that be okay?"

Take control by mentioning less time than they have
"I need to think about it"

"I understand wanting to make a careful decision. What specific aspect of the coverage would you like to think about? I'm here to answer any questions and make sure you have all the information you need."

If they mention specific concern:

"Let me address that specifically... [Answer concern]. Since rates can change daily, would you feel comfortable moving forward if I could address that concern right now?"

If still thinking:

"While you're thinking about it, you're paying more with your current company. We can get the better rate started today and you'll have 30 days to review everything. Would you like to start saving now?"

Always isolate the real concern and address it specifically
"I need to talk to my spouse"

"I completely understand wanting to discuss this with your spouse. Many of our customers say the same thing until they see how much they could save. Let me get you the quote now - that way you'll have specific numbers to discuss. If your spouse has any questions, I can talk to both of you. What's your current rate so I can show you the savings?"

Alternative approach:

"Would it be easier if we got your spouse on a three-way call right now? That way I can answer any questions immediately."

If still resistant:

"Let me ask you this - if I can show you savings of [X] amount per month, would that be worth a quick conversation with your spouse tonight?"

Focus on having concrete numbers to discuss rather than hypotheticals
"Just send me the quote"

"I understand wanting to see everything in writing. Insurance rates change frequently, and I want to make sure you get the best rate possible. Let me quote you right now while I have these rates available. It only takes a few minutes, and I can still email you everything we discuss. Would that be fair?"

If still insisting on email:

"I'll tell you what - let me at least show you the rate while we're on the phone. If you want to think about it, I'll email you everything we discuss. That way, you'll have all the information you need to make a decision. Sound good?"

Create urgency around current rates while offering to still send information
"Your price is too high"

"I understand price is important. Could you help me understand what price point you're comparing to? Sometimes when we look at the coverage levels and available discounts, you might actually be getting more protection for your money."

After they share:

"Let me look at a few more discounts. Are you a homeowner? Do you have any other vehicles we could bundle? Multi-policy discounts often make a big difference."

If still too high:

"We can also look at adjusting some coverage options or deductibles to find a sweet spot that gives you good protection at a better price. What monthly payment would work best for your budget?"

Always focus on value and coverage rather than just price
"I'm still in my six-month policy"

"Actually, you can switch insurance at any time - you're never locked in. When you switch to Allstate, we'll help you cancel your old policy and you'll get a refund for any unused premium. The sooner you switch, the sooner you start saving. Would you like to see how much you could save right now?"

If concerned about refund:

"Your current company is required by law to refund any unused premium. We'll help handle the cancellation to make sure you get your refund. Meanwhile, you'll be paying less with us starting today."

Emphasize there's no need to wait and they're losing money by waiting
"I have a claim on my record"

"Many of our customers have had claims - that's what insurance is for! In fact, we specialize in helping people with claims get better rates. What kind of claim was it and when did it happen? This helps me find the best discounts for your situation."

After they share:

"Thanks for sharing that. The good news is, claims impact your rate less over time, and we have several discounts that could help offset any impact. Let me show you what we can do."

Turn the claim discussion into an opportunity to showcase understanding and expertise
"I won't share my personal information"

"I completely understand your concern about privacy. As a licensed agent, I'm bound by strict privacy laws and use this information solely to ensure you get all available discounts. Your information is protected by law. Which piece of information concerns you most?"

If concerned about DOB:

"Your date of birth helps us apply age-related discounts and ensure accurate coverage. We can't give you the best rate without it. This information is protected by federal privacy laws."

If concerned about address:

"Your address helps us apply regional discounts and assess local factors that could save you money. I can't give you an accurate quote without it. Would you be more comfortable if I explained our privacy policy?"

Always tie information requests to customer benefits
"I'll call you back"

"I understand wanting to call back, but these rates change frequently, and I want to make sure you get the best rate possible. Let me ask you this - if I could save you [amount] per month on better coverage, would you want to know that now or risk losing that rate?"

If still wanting to call back:

"I've got your information pulled up right now. It'll take longer to go through everything if you call back. Since we're already talking, let me at least show you the savings available right now - it only takes a few minutes."

Create urgency around current availability and ease of doing it now
"I'm happy with my current rate"

"That's great that you're satisfied! Many of our current customers said the same thing before saving an average of 20% with us. Since I have you on the phone, let me at least show you what we can offer - it only takes a few minutes, and you might be surprised by the savings while getting better coverage."

If still resistant:

"Just out of curiosity, what would you consider to be a significant enough savings to make a change? Let me see if I can meet or exceed that number."

Turn satisfaction into curiosity about potential savings
"Your deductible is too high"

"I understand your concern about the deductible. We actually have flexibility here. While a higher deductible lowers your monthly payment, we can definitely look at lower deductible options. What amount would you be comfortable with in case of a claim?"

Balance explanation:

"Let me show you how adjusting the deductible affects your monthly payment. Then you can decide which balance works best for your budget."

"Can't afford the down payment"

"I understand that up-front costs can be challenging. We have several flexible payment options. Would you be more comfortable with a smaller down payment spread across your first few months? We can also look at our special first-time customer programs."

Focus on flexibility and payment solutions
"Need to wait for my renewal"

"I hear this often, but actually, you don't need to wait for renewal to switch and save. Insurance can be canceled and started anytime. Your current company is required by law to refund any unused premium. How much are you currently paying monthly?"

If concerned about cancellation:

"We'll help handle the cancellation process completely. Every day you wait is another day you're paying more than you need to. Would you like to see how much you could save starting today?"

Calculate daily savings they're missing by waiting
"Want to wait until next month"

"I understand wanting to wait, but insurance rates can change daily. Let's lock in this rate today, and we can set your payment to start next month. That way you're guaranteed today's savings. Would that work better for you?"

If budget is the concern:

"We can also look at our flexible payment options. Many customers save enough with us to offset any overlapping payments during the switch. What date would work best for your first payment?"

"Not sure about switching companies"

"I understand that changing insurance companies is a big decision. Let me ask you this - what's most important to you in an insurance company? I'd like to show you how Allstate excels in those areas."

Build confidence:

"Allstate has been protecting customers for over 90 years. We have a 97% claim satisfaction rate, and I'm your local agent - always here when you need us. Plus, you get a 30-day look at your policy. What concerns could I address about making the switch?"

Focus on stability, service, and satisfaction guarantee
"Worried about coverage gaps"

"That's a great concern to raise. We specifically time your new policy to start before canceling your old one, ensuring zero gaps in coverage. Would you like me to explain exactly how we handle the transition?"

Detailed explanation:

"We'll start your new policy first, provide you with proof of insurance, then help you cancel your old policy. This ensures you're fully covered throughout the entire process. We can even handle the cancellation for you. Would that give you peace of mind?"

"Getting quotes from other companies"

"I respect doing your research. What specific features are most important in your comparison? I can show you how Allstate excels in those areas, plus our unique benefits that others might not offer."

Competitive advantage:

"While you're comparing, let me lock in today's rate for you. Rates change frequently, and this way you'll have a guaranteed option with all the savings we discussed. There's no obligation, but you'll have this rate secured while you compare. Sound fair?"

Create urgency while respecting their process
"Heard bad things about Allstate"

"I appreciate you sharing that concern. Could you tell me specifically what you've heard? I'd like to address those points directly and share our current customer satisfaction ratings and claims processing statistics."

Share statistics:

"Allstate has a 97% claims satisfaction rate, and we've been protecting customers for over 90 years. Plus, I'm your local agent, always here to help personally. Would you like to hear about our specific guarantees?"

Use specific numbers and personal service to build confidence
"Current company's rewards program"

"I understand valuing reward programs. Let me show you Allstate's Drivewise program, which offers immediate rewards plus safe driving bonuses. Plus, the money you save monthly with us often exceeds annual rewards from other companies. What rewards are most important to you?"

Value comparison:

"If you're saving [amount] monthly with us, that's [amount*12] annually - likely more than your current rewards. Plus, you'll still earn rewards with our program. Would you like to hear more about our rewards structure?"

If No: "When would be a better time for us to talk about saving you money on your insurance?"

"Before I get you quoted for savings, I need to verify a few quick details. For security purposes, could you confirm..."
  • Full name
  • Address (transition to homeowner question)
  • Date of birth (for accurate discounts)
  • Vehicle information

"To make sure I get you the best savings possible..."

  • "What's your typical monthly payment?"

  • Available discounts applied
